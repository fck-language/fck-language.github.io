Why fck?

Built-in localisation

With localisation built into fck from the ground up, your projects can be used by anyone from anywhere

One language, thousands of possibilities. Flavours allow you to use fck for specific tasks. From mathematical evaluation to linguistic analysis, fck flavours have you covered

Compiled and interpreted

With both an LLVM based compiler and interpreter, gone are the days of having to compile test scripts just to check a few commands
