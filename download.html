Specific flavours

What is a flavour?

Flavours of fck use the same language, syntax, etc. The difference is what comes with the language by default. One of the aims of fck was to have a multipurpose language. This means that there are several flavours that have built-in functions, types, etc. based on the flavour you're using.

For example, the counting flavour (for maths) includes several additional types such as matrices, and functions such as the digamma function.
